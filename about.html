Our Story

15 Years of Connecting People with Property

BluePeak Properties was founded in 2011 with a simple belief: buying, selling, or renting a property shouldn't feel transactional. It should feel like working with people who genuinely understand what "home" or "the right investment" means to you.

From a single office in Sandton, we've grown into a full-service agency spanning residential, commercial, and rental property across Gauteng — without losing the personal, advice-first approach we started with.

Our Mission

To make every property transaction transparent, informed, and stress-free — for buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants alike.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's most trusted property brand, known for honest valuations and agents who put clients first.

Our Values

Integrity, local market expertise, and a genuine commitment to finding the right fit — not just the fastest sale.
